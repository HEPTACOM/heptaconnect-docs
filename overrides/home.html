Why HEPTAconnect?

Documentation for various personas
HEPTAconnect packages are developed, deployed, updated, configured and monitored. These tasks are shared across different personas reading the documentation to ensure the expected scope matches the reader.
Open Source your project
The code for every package can be either accessed publicly or with vendor licensing. Work with vendor project support HEPTAhelp or code on your own. HEPTACOM GmbH will enable you to work on your own project.
Host wherever PHP runs
Do not be vendor-locked to a specific hosting environment. Meet your data protection policies and have own DMZ or take a cloud infrastructure provider of your choice.
Update with your other applications
Plan your own maintenance times to match your business actions. No unexpected releases as you have full control of your own project.
Adapt to your business changes
Add new APIs when new services needs to be introduced or cut off old ones. No need to adapt multiple one-trick-data-transfers to work together.
Test-drive
Use code versioning to plan data flows and test them in copies of your infrastructure to ensure your project quality.
Limitless framework
Implement your workflows in your data transfer instead of adapting your workflows to the data transfer software. Connect your data regardless of its structure and availability. Event based triggers, importing files, time based triggers, differential checks, custom checks between APIs.
Prioritize high-load scenarios
Expecting high-load events? Decide which data takes the road and which runs on the speedway. This ensure, that time-relevant data is on time.
Use existing monitoring and backups infrastructure
Place your HEPTAconnect storage anywhere and use your existing backup storage. Make use of health monitoring and integrate your logging services without having to learn new software. Receive emails on any event you need to know about.
